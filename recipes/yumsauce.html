Yum Sauce

Description

Best sauce for grain bowls and dipping

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup silken tofu
  • 1/4 slivered almonds
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder (note: add way more)
  • 2 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except chickpea juice to a blender. Blitz until smooth, slowly adding chickpea juice until you reach a thick, pourable consistency.
