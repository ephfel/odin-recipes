Four Loaves of Bread

Description

From La Dolce Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1/5 cup yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 12 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients, let rise 1 hour
  2. Punch down and knead
  3. Separate into loaves, cover with oil
  4. Let rise 15 minutes in loaf pans
  5. Score the tops, bake 35-38 mins @ 350F
Bon Appetit!
